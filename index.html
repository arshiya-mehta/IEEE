About Us

Welcome to SakshamSetu, an AI-powered platform created to empower people by connecting them with government schemes tailored to their specific needs. Our platform leverages artificial intelligence to simplify access to critical resources, eliminating the challenges of navigating complex initiatives and ensuring less privileged people and farmers receive accurate and relevant information.

People can input their personal, land, and financial details into our user-friendly system, which then analyzes the data to recommend the most suitable schemes. By streamlining this process, SakshamSetu enables the deprived people to focus on growth and sustainability, helping build a stronger and more prosperous India, one farmer at a time.
